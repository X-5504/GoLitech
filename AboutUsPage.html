About GOLITECH

GoLitech is a renowned international manufacturer of computer peripherals, with a global presence through its widespread branches. Specializing in cutting-edge gaming gear, GoLitech caters to the needs of avid gamers by offering an extensive range of high-quality products. From precision gaming mice to immersive headsets and responsive keyboards, GoLitech is dedicated to enhancing the gaming experience for enthusiasts worldwide. With a commitment to innovation and advanced technology, the company consistently delivers products that meet the demands of competitive gamers and casual players alike. As a leader in the industry, GoLitech strives to provide exceptional performance, durability, and ergonomic design in all its offerings. Whether in professional eSports arenas or casual gaming setups, GoLitech is the trusted brand that empowers gamers to elevate their gameplay to new heights.

Our Mission

At GoLitech, our mission is to empower gamers worldwide by providing them with cutting-edge gaming gear that enhances their gaming experience. We strive to develop innovative and high-quality computer peripherals that enable gamers to unlock their full potential and achieve their gaming goals. History

January 2023 • Launch of Gaming Gear Innovation Labs

GoLitech established state-of-the-art Gaming Gear Innovation Labs to push the boundaries of gaming technology. These labs served as hubs for research and development, where GoLitech's engineers and designers worked tirelessly to create next-generation gaming peripherals. The company's focus on innovation enabled it to introduce groundbreaking features such as advanced sensor technology, customizable RGB lighting, and ergonomic designs, setting new industry standards.


September 2021 • Collaborations with Game Developers

GoLitech embarked on strategic collaborations with prominent game developers to create exclusive and customized gaming gear. These collaborations aimed to combine GoLitech's expertise in crafting high-performance peripherals with the unique aesthetics and features of popular game titles. By working closely with developers, GoLitech ensured that its products were optimized for specific games, providing gamers with an immersive and seamless gaming experience.


March 2020 • "Go Green with GoLitech"

Continuing its sustainability efforts, GoLitech introduced the "Go Green with GoLitech" initiative. As part of this campaign, the company implemented various environmentally friendly practices throughout its operations. These included energy-efficient manufacturing processes, waste reduction measures, and responsible packaging. GoLitech also partnered with environmental organizations to promote eco-conscious gaming and organized community clean-up events to inspire gamers to take an active role in preserving the environment.


October 2018 • Sustainable Materials Campaign

GoLitech launched a groundbreaking campaign focused on sustainability and environmental responsibility. Recognizing the need to reduce its ecological footprint, GoLitech made a bold commitment to utilize recycled and refined materials in the production of its gaming gear. This campaign aimed to raise awareness among gamers about the importance of sustainable practices and their positive impact on the planet.


May 2015 • Game Tournament Sponsorship

GoLitech has been actively supporting the gaming community by sponsoring prestigious game tournaments around the world. Starting in May 2015, GoLitech became a prominent sponsor of major eSports events, providing top-of-the-line gaming gear for participants and ensuring an exceptional gaming experience. By partnering with renowned tournaments, GoLitech demonstrates its commitment to fostering competitive gaming and supporting the aspirations of talented gamers worldwide.

Background Story

In the early 2000s, a group of passionate gamers came together with a shared vision to create a company dedicated to gaming peripherals. They believed that gamers deserved cutting-edge technology and exceptional gaming experiences. With unwavering determination, they started in a small garage, tirelessly designing and developing innovative gaming gear. Their dedication paid off as word spread about the precision and performance of their prototypes. Gamers were captivated, and demand for their products grew rapidly. GoLitech established its headquarters, focusing on refining their offerings based on gamer feedback. Their commitment to quality, innovation, and user experience set them apart from competitors. As their product portfolio expanded to include gaming mice, headsets, and keyboards, gamers trusted GoLitech to enhance their gameplay. With a growing global presence, GoLitech opened branches worldwide, serving gamers across continents. Their customer-centric approach and relentless pursuit of innovation made them an international leader in gaming peripherals. Today, GoLitech continues to push the boundaries of gaming technology, driven by their original vision and passion for gaming. They remain at the forefront, empowering gamers to achieve new levels of performance and enjoyment.
